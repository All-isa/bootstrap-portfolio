About Me


My name is Allisa, I am originally from San Diego, California. I am an aspiring web developer, collector of moments through photography, and mother to a rambunctious two year old. Currently I am residing in Houston, Texas. I am driven by my passion to constantly do better, for not only myself, but to hopefully make a larger impact in some way. I love a good challenge and enjoy constantly learning something new. My hobbies include any sort of outdoor exercise (hiking, biking, surfing), photography, reading, and exploring new places from my neighborhood to another country.
